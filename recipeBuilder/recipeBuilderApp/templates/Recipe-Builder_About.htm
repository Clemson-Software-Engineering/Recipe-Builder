About Recipe-Builder

Using Kroger's API, our team has created this open source app in order to make grocery shopping easier than ever before. Recipe-Builder is designed to allow anyone to input a recipe they've found or love, as well as their current location. In return, the app will then show them the location and price of each of the items in their recipe at their nearest Kroger store.

As new features are constantly being added to Recipe-Builder, future grocery shoppers can expect to be able to create their own accounts and even save all of their favorite recipes for later use. No more wasting time searching for lost recipes!
